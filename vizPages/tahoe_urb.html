Urbanization in Tahoe Basin: Impacts & Data Collection

Through ArcGIS Online, Story Maps, ArcGIS Developer and ArcGIS Collector, I developed an analysis on the impact of urbanization on the ecological integrity of Tahoe Basin. I performed analysis in ArcGIS online, and displayed my data through a Story Map, which displays background information, symbolized and formatted analyses, and recommended steps forward for conservation. I created my own web layers in ArcGIS Developer, and imported them into the Story Map, which highlight areas of conservation, as well as newly collected field data. Additionally, the field data will be continually collected through ArcGIS Collector and retroactively updated in the Story Map.

Methods Used: ArcGIS Online, Story Maps, ArcGIS Developer, ArcGIS Collector
